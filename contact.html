About Location

The gym at Michigan State University, known as the IM West building, is located in East Lansing, Michigan. Situated at the heart of the university's campus, IM West can be found at the intersection of Chestnut Road and West Shaw Lane. The gym offers state of the art facilities for physical fitness and wellness, including a variety of exercise equipment, indoor courts for basketball and volleyball, swimming pools, and group fitness studios.
